Teaching

Teaching Philosophy

I approach teaching as a dialogical, collaborative endeavor that empowers students to think critically, and engage thoughtfully with the world around them. The liberal arts tradition emphasizes close reading, open dialogue, and the development of independent thought.

I believe that education should not merely transmit knowledge but inspire curiosity and a willingness to question assumptions.
