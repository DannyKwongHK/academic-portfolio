## Teaching Philosophy

I approach teaching as a dialogical, collaborative endeavor that empowers students to think critically and engage thoughtfully with the world around them. The liberal arts model guides my belief that education should challenge assumptions while fostering intellectual curiosity and social awareness.

I believe that education should not merely transmit knowledge but inspire curiosity and a willingness to question assumptions.

## Teaching Experience

I have led tutorials as a teaching assistant for the following courses:

- **The Making of Hong Kong** - Spring 2024
- **Social Gerontology** - Fall 2023
- **Global Health** - Spring 2023
- **Social Welfare and Social Problems in Hong Kong** - Spring 2023
- **Classical Sociological Theory** - Fall 2022
- **Introduction to Sociology** - Fall 2022

My approach emphasizes active participation, critical thinking, and real-world applications of sociological concepts.
