Competitive Yo-Yoing

Outside of Academia, you can find me in the scene of competitive yo-yoing. I started competitive yo-yoing in 2015, and am now transitting from contestant to organizer. I establish the Hong Kong Yoyo Organization as an offical body for yo-yoing in Hong Kong. You can find out more through the following links.

The biggest challenge faced by the yo-yoing community is the lack of venue for organizing competitions. If you have related networks to performance venues in Hong Kong at a reasonable price, please reach out to me.
